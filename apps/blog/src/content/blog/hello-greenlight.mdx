This is the sample blog that ships with the Greenlight baseline. It exists to prove
the **deploy → verify → promote** loop end-to-end on a real, low-stakes artifact.

When you clone Greenlight and run `greenlight init`, this becomes *your* blog: swap
the domain, write your own posts under `src/content/blog/`, and ship them through the
same loop the rest of your tools use.
