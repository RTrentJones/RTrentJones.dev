I'm Trent — a senior software engineer at Amazon, with a degree in neuroscience hiding behind the
computer science one. There's more [about me](/about/) if you're curious.

I don't know yet how much I'll write here. The intent is one centralized place for my explorations
in software engineering — and whatever else I get curious about, which tends to drift toward biology
and science.

For most of my career I rarely found the time to take a side project far enough to be worth sharing.
Agentic, AI-aided development changed that math: I can chase an idea to something real in the spare
hours I actually have, and explore a lot more along the way. The things that came out of that are
what this site is here to keep track of.

More soon — I'll flesh this out as I go.
